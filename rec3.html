Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the skillet. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
