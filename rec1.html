Vegetarian Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas)
  • 1 block tofu, cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cups cooked rice

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the vegetables and tofu, toss to combine.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.
