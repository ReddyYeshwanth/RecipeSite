Recipe 2: Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Brush with olive oil.
  2. Grill chicken on medium-high heat until fully cooked, about 6-8 minutes per side. Let it rest before slicing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add sliced grilled chicken on top of the salad.
  5. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
